Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

What is a URL shortner?

A URL shortener converts long, complex web addresses (like https://example.com/very/long/path?param=value ) into shorter, easier-to-share links (like short.ly/abc123). It's perfect for social media, messaging, or printing.

Is it free to use?

Yes! Our basic URL shortening service is 100% free for all users. Premium features (like custom domains, analytics, or link expiration) may require a subscription.

Do shortened links expire?
  • Free links: Never expire (unless they violate our Terms of Service ).
  • Custom expiration: Premium users can set links to auto-delete after a date, clicks, or time period.
Are my links secure?
  • Absolutely! We:
  • ✅ Scan all URLs for malware/phishing.
  • ✅ Block links to illegal or harmful content.
  • ✅ Use HTTPS encryption for all shortened links.
  • Note: Avoid shortening sensitive URLs (e.g., password reset links)
Are there usage limits?
  • Free tier: 2 links/min, No-Info clicks/day.
  • Premium: Unlimited links, clicks, and features.
